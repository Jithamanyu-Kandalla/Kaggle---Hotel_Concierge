Dialogflow Intent Setup:
Manually on Dialogflow console:

Intent Name		Training Phrases Examples			Parameters and Entities
BookRoom		"I want to book a single room on 2025-11-29"	room-type (entity: @room-type), date (@sys.date)
CheckAvailability	"Is a double room available on 2025-12-01?"	room-type, date
ExplainServices		"What services do you offer?"			None
CancelBooking		"Cancel my booking for 2025-12-05"		(Optional parameters for cancellation)

Make sure the parameter names you pick in Dialogflow match those used in the code: room-type and date.

For fulfillment:
Enable webhook fulfillment for these intents and use your local ngrok URL followed by /webhook when testing locally.
